Cost of Living Index Analysis
Analyzing Trends in Everyday living Costs

Monthly Expense Breakdown (in %)

The monthly expense breakdown shows the percentage distribution of common living expenses.
Housing takes up the largest portion of the monthly budget, followed by food and transportation.
Food and Transportation follow closely behind, indicating significant expenditures in these areas.
Utilities and Healthcare make up a smaller but still notable chunk of the monthly expenses.
The Others category represents a relatively minor portion of the overall budget.

Housing Cost Comparison Across Cities (in ₹)

* Mumbai has the highest average monthly rent, significantly higher than the other cities.
